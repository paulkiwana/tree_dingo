About The Guardians of Nature

Welcome to Tree Dingo Animations, home of "The Guardians of Nature" - an exciting webcomic series about Blaze, the Guardian of fire, on his quest to find the other guardians of nature: water, Terra, energy, flora, and air.


Follow along as Blaze meets new friends and faces challenges in this adventure-filled story. The comic updates regularly with new episodes that continue to expand this magical world of elemental guardians.


Created with passion and dedication, this series aims to bring joy and excitement to readers of all ages. Thank you for joining us on this incredible journey!
